# Scholars of the Mythic Stratum

## Origin

The Mystics never built keeps; they built **observatories**. While
the medieval realms were drawing borders and the orc clans were
binding the Ember layer, a quiet network of scholars and ascetics
began climbing the strata in the other direction — *up* — toward the
**Mythic Stratum**, the topmost layer where the residual will of
forgotten gods still flickers.

They learned that the strata are not just sedimentary rock and
locked magic. They are *answers*. Every stratum is a sentence the
land once said. The Mythic Stratum is the land's last sentence —
the one still being composed.

The mystic-archetype factions are the descendants of those scholars.
They wear robes the colour of the Mythic Stratum's pulse (a desaturated
violet-rose). They build domed observatories instead of keeps. They
maintain *sanctuaries* — hex-tiles ceded permanently to the strata,
unworked, unbuilt-on.

## Disposition

**Knowledge-hoarding.** A mystic-archetype faction wants to **learn**
the strata, not exploit them. They will trade secrets in exchange for
sanctuaries; they will withdraw from a war rather than break a
sanctuary; they will side with the undead to reseal a breach if the
breach is *interesting enough to study afterward*.

A mystic AI tends to:

- Open with a sanctuary declaration over its best-rolled tile.
- Prioritise Discoveries (research) over military.
- Propose pacts with everyone in the first 60s — pact = "leave my
  sanctuaries alone, I leave your keeps alone".
- Strongly prefer the `coexistence` mode (knowledge-hoarding +
  isolation). <!-- M_V11.PURGE — age-of-strata mode removed -->

## Signature units + buildings

| Unit / Building | Lore role |
|---|---|
| **Town Hall** | An **observatory** — a hex-domed scholar's tower. No brazier; the centre is a strata-resonance plate that reads the Mythic Stratum's pulse. |
| **Footman (warden variant)** | A robed sanctuary-guardian armed with a quarterstaff. Slow, durable, refuses to attack a faction that hasn't broken a sanctuary. |
| **Watchtower** | A second observatory — sees by reading the strata's pulse change, not by line of sight. Effective in fog. |
| **Wall** | Rare. Mystic walls are *meditation walls* — they slow movement but don't block. |
| **Wonder** | The Codex — a permanent record of one new sentence of the Mythic Stratum. Other realms can read it (unlocks a Discovery they don't have). |

## Banner

Mythic-violet on cream, the field charged with three vertical bars
(strata cross-section sigil) and a single high dot (the Mythic
Stratum).

## In-game flavour hooks

- **Procedural nickname**: motifs are *Codex*, *Sanctuary*, *Vigil*,
  *Pulse*, *Quiet*. Example: "THE QUIET CODEX" for a mystic win
  via coexistence + a Wonder.
- **MYTH event reactions**: treated as *signal*. Even disasters are
  data. The mystics will read a wildfire's smoke pattern for
  meaning.
- **Diplomacy**: chatters constantly but says little. Treaties
  always include a sanctuary clause.
