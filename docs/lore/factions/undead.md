# Revenants of the Glacial Halt

## Origin

Before the charter, a charter-claimant realm was caught **mid-march**
when a Glacial-Stratum event froze their column of land solid in
hours. Every footman, every horse, every banner — held in place. The
charter signatories of the time treated the site as cursed and drew
the hex-grid AROUND it.

Then, centuries later, charter-realms began to *break the seal* —
mining shafts came too close, walls were rooted too deep, wonders
were sited atop the frozen column. The Glacial Stratum, disturbed,
**woke the march**. The revenants rose still in formation, still
holding their orders, still expecting to relieve a keep that no
longer existed.

The undead archetype is what those revenants and the strata-bound
fragments around them have become — *reclamation walking*. Their
goal is not conquest. It is **reseal**.

## Disposition

**Reclamation.** An undead-archetype faction treats every other
faction as *the disturbance*. They want to push the living back
across a memorial line — the boundary of the original frozen march
— and then settle. Wars are not for territory; they are for **quiet**.

An undead AI tends to:

- Build defensively for the first half of a match while regenerating
  its march-formation.
- Move slowly but with terrible discipline; once the formation is
  full, it advances in lock-step.
- Refuse most diplomatic overtures from medieval factions (the heirs
  of the realm that broke the seal). Accept overtures from mystic
  factions (who at least *understand*).
- Treat barbarian camps as kin — undirected fragments of the same
  Glacial pain. May refuse to attack them.

## Signature units + buildings

| Unit / Building | Lore role |
|---|---|
| **Town Hall** | A re-roofed memorial cairn over the original frozen marching column. The brazier is cold. |
| **Footman** | Still in oath-formation, eight centuries late. Carries the rusted standard of a realm that no longer exists. |
| **Watchtower** | A bone-and-frost pillar that watches the reseal line. |
| **Wall** | Built from re-frozen Glacial-Stratum dust. Heals slowly while the strata are quiet. |
| **Wonder** | The Reseal — an architectural promise to never disturb the layers below again. |

## Banner

Frost-pale blue on charcoal, the field charged with a broken
hour-glass sigil.

## In-game flavour hooks

- **Procedural nickname**: motifs are *Halt*, *Reseal*, *Memorial*,
  *Frost*, *Standard*. Example: "THE PATIENT HALT" for an undead
  win via outlasting a medieval rush.
- **MYTH event reactions**: every event is treated as confirmation
  that the strata are sick and the disturbance must end.
- **Diplomacy**: silent for most of a match. When they speak, they
  speak in surveyor's language ("withdraw to the memorial line").
