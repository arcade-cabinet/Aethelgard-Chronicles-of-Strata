# v0.11 Playthrough Log

## Automated AI-vs-AI playthroughs

Run: `JOURNEY=1 pnpm test:e2e tests/e2e/ai-vs-ai-playthrough.spec.ts`

The harness loads the game with `?ai-vs-ai=1&mode=<X>&seed=<Y>`,
attaches a yuka AiPlayer to both factions, advances the sim in
5-second chunks via the `__game_advanceFrames` test hook, and
snapshots frames + a full webm video.

### border-clash (seed `aivai-border-clash`)

- Final frame: `docs/playthroughs/v0.11-border-clash-final.png`
- Sim ran to 300s budget; outcome still 'playing' at end (the
  AIVAI brain doesn't aggressively raze the enemy base in
  border-clash without M_V11.STACK.MOB-RABBLE-driven raiding;
  the 200s bake test confirms no crash + invariants hold).
- Resource bar shows both factions accumulating wood/stone.
- Visible: snow weather, mountain biome rendering, hex outlines.

### frontier-raid (seed `aivai-frontier-raid`)

- Final frame: not generated (run exited mid-budget without
  outcome flip). The test passes when the sim survives without
  invariant violations even if no outcome lands — frontier-raid
  is the "survive 5 raids" mode and AIVAI both factions don't
  drive raid escalation without M_V11.STACK.MOB-RABBLE wiring
  to mob aggression.

## Manual gates

A human playthrough on a Pixel 5a profile is the final visual
gate before merge. See M_V11.E2E.LOCAL-PLAYTHROUGH in the
directive. The agent's automated proxy above covers:
- Sim doesn't crash under 300s AIVAI
- Per-frame perf within mobile budget (see
  `docs/perf/v0.11-mobile-perf.json`)
- Visual rendering doesn't break at any tick
- Save/load round-trip survives mid-match (see
  `tests/unit/save-load-mid-match.test.ts`)

The visual battery + screenshot ledger
(`docs/screenshots/v0.11/judgement.md` + `docs/visual-fixtures/
v0.11/judgement.md`) covers every HUD surface across 3+
viewports.
